Bringing pintodog.com into the 21st century

1st of May 2021

Over the last weeek or so, I have set about modernising the entireity of this website. I even sorted out the blog!

Some of the new features available are:

  • A revamped blog section, with a fancy layout thing, powered by CSS;
  • A revamped homepage;
  • Linktrees on every page, showing the location of a page within the website;
  • A new PintoCraft homepage;
  • A new seperate PintoCraft history page;
  • A new set of news pages, again for PintoCraft;
  • A new Projects page, where I will put downloads and summaries of my current projects;
  • And on the backend, all CSS and JS files have been unified, removing duplicates and saving space.
