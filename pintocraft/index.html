How to join PintoCraft

If you want to join PintoCraft, first you must be invited by an existing member, and then you need to pass an application process. If you have been invited by a friend, please click here to join the Discord server, where you will find the #get-whitelisted channel. Enter the commad p!profile create [username] within this channel. The bot will then ask you a few questions, and your answers will the sent to the Admin Team, who should look through your application and then either accept or deny it within 24 hours.

FAQ

Q: Can I join on Bedrock/Console/Pocket Edition?

A: We run a Java edition serever, but we used to have Xbox, Windows 10 (bedrock) and Pocket edition support, through a proxy called Geyser. However, due to low utilisation and high resource costs we removed it for PintoCraft season 3. However, it may return in the future, if there is demand for it.

Q: Can I join if I don't know anyone/haven't been invited?

A: No. In order to preserve the small community-like atmosphere of the server, you can only join if you have been invited by a current member.

Q: Can I join if I don't have a PC?

A: Yes! A lot of us play on our laptops that we use for school and you can play on any computer that runs Windows, Mac, Linux, or even Chrome OS.

History Of PintoCraft

PintoCraft has now been running contiuously for over a year. We have had 3 complete seasons in that time, with the fourth still ongoing. When we started out we had 5 players, but now we have an active discord with over 30, and is set to expand more when PintoCraft Season 5 releases this summer.
