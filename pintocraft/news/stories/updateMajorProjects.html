The Super Honest Association of Money (SHAM)

The newest (and only) casino on the server, currently offering a slot machine (see image below), with a shooting gallery, stat poker table, a new and innovative cryptocurrency service, and much more currently under construction

Project DELTA

The Project DELTA enabling works are now mostly complete, with the current portfolio of farms consiting of: A Gnembon 10x Iron farm, a Gnembon Slime farm, a Gnembon gold farm, an ImpulseSV villager breeder, and an Ilmango Witch farm. These all produce all the raw materials that are needed to build the network of HyperLinks. As for the HyperLinks themselves, the tunnels for them are currently under construction, with the initial route running from the Shopping District to the Fishing Village and the Project DELTA industrial district, passing by Project Gemma, and JimmyTheMilkMan's base.
